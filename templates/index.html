About Us

This is a research funded by Indian Council of Medical Research (New Delhi). The team comprises of a renowned figure in the field of positive psychology, currently serving as an Assistant professor of psychology in Birla Institute of Technology and Science, a clinical psychologist, interested in the area of psychotherapy, currently serving as a senior research fellow in Birla Institute of Technology and Science, and two immensely talented and budding computer professionals devoted their effort in the technical part of the website. With all the diversities and expertise in their respective field, this team is dedicated to answer the pertaining question of this study which revolves around the enhancement of wellbeing among youths. With careful selection of four domains (self management, stress management, emotional intelligence and time management) this would be a novel effort to combine the individual factors of wellbeing under one roof.

Lastly we thank you immensely for your participation in this study. We need your support and cooperation to make this research happen.
