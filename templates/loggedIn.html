About Us

This is a research funded by Indian Council of Medical Research (New Delhi). The team comprises of a renowned figure in the field of positive psychology, currently serving as an Assistant professor of psychology in Birla Institute of Technology and Science, a clinical psychologist, interested in the area of psychotherapy, currently serving as a senior research fellow in Birla Institute of Technology and Science, and two immensely talented and budding computer professionals devoted their effort in the technical part of the website. With all the diversities and expertise in their respective field, this team is dedicated to answer the pertaining question of this study which revolves around the enhancement of wellbeing among youths. With careful selection of four domains (self management, stress management, emotional intelligence and time management) this would be a novel effort to combine the individual factors of wellbeing under one roof.

Lastly we thank you immensely for your participation in this study. We need your support and cooperation to make this research happen.

Self Management

Self-management represents a collection of skills that individuals can engage in order to become more self-reliant. in order to manage self, one has to have a clear understanding of self-selecting targets known as goals and also the required skills to achieve a particular target(s). overall self-management is a key to performance, health, productivity and satisfaction with your life and career.

Emotional Intelligence

Emotional intelligence is the ability to understand and recognize your emotions also understanding of how your emotions affect people around you. The major portion of your success is contributed by emotional intelligence..

Time Management

Time management means giving insight into time consuming activities, changing time expenditure and increasing efficiency by teaching people how to make a daily planning, how to prioritize task and handle unexpected tasks. this exercise is supposed to be helpful to the students in enhancing their skills for time management.

Stress Management

The present era is characterized by competition which demands increased awareness about stress and stressors, the inability of which may lead to a cascade of negative consequences. These exercises will bring awareness so that you can be able to cope up with your stressor in an effective manner.
